Police Department FAQ

🎫 How do I pay a speeding ticket?

Traffic citations are paid at the Union County Clerk of Court — not the police department. For more information, call (641) 782-8402.

📄 How do I get a police report?

Come to the Police Department at 302 N Pine Street. Reports are free for involved parties. Non-involved parties pay $5. Call (641) 782-8402.

🐾 Who handles stray animals?

Animal Control operates Mon–Fri 7 AM–4 PM. Call the Police Department at (641) 782-8402 to dispatch Animal Control.

📜 How do I get a protection order?

You must go through the Clerk of Court to file for a protection order. Call 911 if you are in immediate danger.

❄️ What is the Snow Emergency Ordinance?

For details on Creston's Snow Emergency Ordinance and parking restrictions during snow emergencies, call (641) 782-8402.

🏛️ How do I apply for a building permit?

Building permit applications are handled at Creston City Hall, 116 W Adams Street. Contact City Hall for current fees and processing times.
