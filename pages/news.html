1776 Firehouse & Lounge Opens at 301 W. Adams

Mayor Waylon Clayton and wife Kylie opened the 1776 Firehouse & Lounge in the historic Berning Motor Inn building. The restaurant serves steaks, BBQ, pasta, and elevated cocktails, with Clayton calling it "a dream" and saying "the people are crying out for their town to get these things back."

Anson's Bar & Grill Opens at 211 W Adams — Chamber Ribbon Cutting

Joe Anson reopened the long-time Uptown Creston restaurant at 211 W Adams Street under new ownership and a new name. The Chamber of Commerce held an official ribbon cutting, with Director Ellen Gerharz welcoming the Anson family as new business owners.

Balloon Days 2024 Wraps — Another Successful Year for Iowa's 2nd Largest Festival

The 37th annual Creston/Southwest Iowa Balloon Days festival concluded to strong turnout. The NightGlow drew thousands and the Saturday parade featured high school marching bands from across the region. Planning for 2025 already underway.

4th of July Draws Thousands to Creston — Street Dance, Fireworks Dazzle Crowd

Creston's legendary 4th of July celebration drew visitors from across southwest Iowa and northern Missouri. The July 3rd street dance set the tone and the fireworks show on Independence Day drew praise from all corners of the region.

Creston Mural Project Passes 50 — New Works Added to Uptown

Creston's Mural Magic project continued to grow, with new international and regional muralists contributing to the city's visual landscape. The project now counts over 50 murals citywide with 30 in the concentrated Uptown corridor.
